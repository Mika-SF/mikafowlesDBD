Introduction to Big Data Technologies and Data Management

This unit explored big data and associated technologies as well as concepts underlying data management and how these technologies effectively manipulate big data within an organisation.

In this unit, I learned:

  • Features and characteristics of big data (Review).
  • Exponential growth of data, boundlessness and high complexity of data.
  • Evaluation of the 4Vs of big data.
  • Critical evaluation of methodologies, tools, techniques and strategies.
  • Data security requirements.
