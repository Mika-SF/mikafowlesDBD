Data Cleaning and Transformation

This unit introduced concepts, techniques and methods of data cleaning and transformation.

Unit 4 covers:

  • The data cleaning and transformation concepts.
  • Analysing the data management pipeline.
  • Evaluating factors affecting data cleaning.
  • Requirements critical for the data design and process automation.

Learning Outcomes:

  • To clean and transform data using the data pipeline as a guide.
  • To examine and understand factors that affect data cleaning.
  • To understand requirements for design automation.
