Compliance and Regulatory Framework for Managing Data

This Unit investigated compliance frameworks regarding data management, specifically focusing on the rights of individuals and the obligations of organisations to their stakeholders.

In this unit, I learned:

  • Analysing compliance obligations for data stakeholders.
  • Examining rights accorded to individuals with respect to data usage.
  • Understanding applicable standards.
  • Examining existing regulatory frameworks.
  • Evaluating activities that require regulation.
  • Reviewing organisations and industry affected by regulation.
